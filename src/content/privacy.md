# Privacy Policy - Aerodesk

**Last Updated**: December 3, 2025

## Overview

Aerodesk is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension collects, uses, and protects your information.

---

## What We Collect

### Data Stored Locally (On Your Computer)
- Organization identifier for authentication
- GDS session tokens and cookies
- Command history and booking tasks
- Team member configuration
- Extension preferences and settings

**All data is stored locally on your computer** and is not sent anywhere unless explicitly configured.

### What We DO NOT Collect

- ❌ Your GDS password
- ❌ Passenger personal information
- ❌ Payment or credit card details
- ❌ Your browsing history outside GDS
- ❌ Cross-site tracking data

---

## Data Protection

### Local Storage
- Data is stored securely in your browser
- Protected by your operating system
- Automatically deleted when you uninstall the extension

### Communication Security
- GDS communication uses HTTPS encryption
- All data transmission is encrypted
- No unencrypted credentials transmitted

### Access Control
- Requires organization identifier to authenticate
- Each user's data is isolated
- Team members only access their organization

---

## Data Retention & Deletion

| Data | Storage | Deletion |
|------|---------|----------|
| Command Logs | Local Browser | Manual or automatic |
| Booking Tasks | Local Browser | Delete from interface |
| Auth Tokens | Browser Storage | Logout or uninstall |

**Automatic Deletion**: Uninstalling the extension clears all stored data.
